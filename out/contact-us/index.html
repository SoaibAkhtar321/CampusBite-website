Frequently Asked

When is the app launching?

CampusBite is currently in development. Follow us or sign up to get notified when we launch in your region.

Which campuses can use CampusBite?

Any closed-campus ecosystem — colleges, universities, hostels, corporate campuses, hospitals, and society campuses.

How do I register my food outlet?

Reach out to us at support.campusbite@gmail.com or call us. Our team will guide you through the vendor onboarding process.

Is there a fee to partner?

Onboarding details and pricing will be shared during the partnership discussion. Contact us to know more.
