Frequently Asked

What is CampusBite?

CampusBite is a campus food ordering platform that helps students order food from campus vendors, reduce waiting time, and manage pickup more easily.

When is the app launching?

CampusBite is currently in MVP development and testing. We are preparing it for campus-level launch and Play Store release.

Which campuses can use CampusBite?

CampusBite is designed for colleges, universities, hostels, corporate campuses, hospitals, and other closed-campus food ecosystems.

How do I register my food outlet?

Reach out to us at support.campusbite@gmail.com or call us. Our team will guide you through the vendor onboarding process.

How can I get support for an order or payment?

Email us with your registered phone number, order details, payment proof, and a clear explanation of the issue.
